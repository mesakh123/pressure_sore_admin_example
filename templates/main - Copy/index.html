        {%trans 'According to WHO statistics, about 265,000 people died from burns and scalds wound every year. In the United States itself, about 10,000,000 peoples go to the emergency department for burns and scalds wound every year.         {% blocktrans %}Burns and scalds are accidents that closely related to our daily lives. It can be caused by cooking or taking a bath. Effective treatment of burns and scalds require accurate judgment of the area and depth of burns and scalds. However, this requires a lot of clinical experience. Fortunately, there are instruments that can be used to diagnose the depth of burns and scalds, such as laser Duppler and infrared Thermal imaging camera, etc.; However, the judgment of burn and scald area is still estimated by visual observation. Therefore, documents in various countries have pointed out (Harish V, Burns. 2015. Baartmans MG, Injury. 2012.), this method makes emergency or Outpatient doctors and plastic surgeons have very different estimates of burn area. Not only that, but different burn experts also have very different estimation of the same patient (Giretzlehner M, Burns. 2013). Knowing accurately the area of scald or burn wound is important because it is related to the amount of fluids given, the nutrients given, whether the intensive care unit is needed, and to know whether the hospitals are able to take care of them or not.{%endblocktrans%}

        {% trans "In order to solve this problem, we collected thousands of images of burns and scalds and labeled those images by many of plastic surgeons. Then we used multiple deep learning algorithms and trained models to automatically diagnose various depths' area. The extent of burns and scalds. In order to calculate the area of burns and scalds, we have also trained other models to accurately determine the palm area. Since the palm (excluding fingers) is 0.5% of the total body surface area in children or adults. By recording the patient’s wound and palm at the same or known photographing distance, the burn area can be calculated using an artificial intelligence model." %}
